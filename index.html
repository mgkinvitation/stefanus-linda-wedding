Our Story in Memories

Love Story

The Beautiful Journey We've Shared

  • Introduction

    "When We First Met"
    Before we met and got to this point, we had actually known each other for a long time, since our middle school days. However, we went to different schools and were just acquaintances. After that, we graduated and lost contact.

  • Love Is In The Air

    "The Moment Our Hearts Became One"
    In 2021, Stefanus started searching for Linda’s contact, because, all this time, he hadn’t found anyone who shared the same faith. He remembered an old friend who did, and began searching for Linda's name on social media, but to no avail. One day, since he worked at a delivery company, he asked a courier who regularly delivered packages to Linda's area for help, and finally, he succeeded in finding her.

  • Forever After

    "Stepping into Forever"
    Finally, in 2023, we started a relationship because we were working together to manage Stefanus' online business. We began saving money, and once our savings were enough, we decided to get married in November 2024.
